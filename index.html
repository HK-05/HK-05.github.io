About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Graduate School of AI, UNIST, advised by Prof. Sung Whan Yoon. My research builds robust reinforcement learning on the geometry of the reward landscape — establishing theoretical links between flatness and policy robustness — and develops quantum machine learning algorithms that address the fundamental limitations of classical machine learning.

I am currently a research intern on the Automated Driving team at Qualcomm AI Research, working on world model–based closed-loop RL for robust end-to-end driving. Before my Ph.D., I served as a Republic of Korea Navy as an AI specialist officer, working across AI education, AI system development and deployment, and defense AI policy.
